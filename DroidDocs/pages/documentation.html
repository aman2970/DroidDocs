AppsFlyer Deeplinks

AppsFlyer deep linking is a feature that enables marketers and app developers to create customized user experiences by directing users to specific in-app content or actions based on the context of their interactions.

AppsFlyer In-App-Events

In-app events are actions or occurrences within a mobile application that indicate user engagement, such as making a purchase, completing a level, or subscribing to a service. AppsFlyer allows developers to track and measure these events accurately, enabling them to assess user engagement, retention, and monetization strategies effectively.

Firebase Cloud Messaging

Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) is a robust platform provided by Google for sending push notifications to mobile devices. These notifications play a crucial role in engaging users and keeping them informed about important updates or events within an application.

Bluetooth Classic Integration

Bluetooth Classic, often referred to simply as Bluetooth, is a wireless communication technology that enables devices to connect and communicate over short distances. Developed by Bluetooth SIG (Special Interest Group), Bluetooth Classic has been a cornerstone of wireless connectivity since its introduction.

Bluetooth Low Energy Integration

Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), also known as Bluetooth Smart, is a wireless communication technology designed for low-power devices and applications. Developed by Bluetooth SIG (Special Interest Group), BLE offers significant advantages for the Internet of Things (IoT) and other battery-powered devices.

OpenStreet Maps

OpenStreetMap (OSM) is a collaborative project that aims to create a free, editable map of the world. Founded in 2004 by Steve Coast, OSM has grown into one of the largest and most comprehensive mapping initiatives globally, driven by contributions from volunteers around the world.

Posted on:25 May 2024

YouTube Player Library

Android-youtube-player is a stable and customizable open source YouTube player for Android. It provides a simple View that can be easily integrated in every Activity/Fragment. The library is a wrapper around the IFrame Player API, which runs inside a WebView. Therefore, there are no issues with YouTube Terms of Service. This library also provides a Chromecast YouTube player, that you can use to cast YouTube videos from your app to a Chromecast device.
